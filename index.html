Introduction

Hello, my name is Baki Hanma, but you can call me Baki. I’m from Tokyo, Japan, and I’m currently 18 years old. My birthday is September 4, 2000.

I don’t attend a regular school—instead, I dedicate my life to intense martial arts training and underground fighting. I’m the youngest champion of the Tokyo Dome Underground Arena, where only the strongest warriors compete in secret no-rules matches.

My mother’s name is Emi Akezawa, a wealthy woman who raised me to be strong, hoping I would impress my father. My father is Yuujirou Hanma, also known as “The Ogre”, considered the strongest creature on Earth. He’s my biggest rival and the reason I strive to grow stronger every day. I also have a half-brother named Jack Hanma, who shares my father’s blood and the same drive to surpass him.

I’ve been training in martial arts since childhood. I’m skilled in many disciplines including grappling, boxing, karate, and judo, and I’ve developed superhuman strength, speed, endurance, and reflexes. I can even manipulate my brain chemicals to boost my performance in battle.

My hobbies include combat training, meditation, and challenging powerful opponents. I rarely rest because I believe that pain, pressure, and hardship are the keys to becoming stronger. I also enjoy simple pleasures like quiet moments, time with friends, and occasionally training in nature or extreme environments.

“To become stronger, you must embrace the pain. Only through suffering does power awaken.” – Baki Hanma
